= echo-types Wiki
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 2

[.lead]
*echo-types* is a constructive Agda formalisation of fiber-based *structured
loss* — "echo types". The central definition is

[source,agda]
----
Echo f y := Σ (x : A) , (f x ≡ y)
----

the proof-relevant fiber of `f` over `y`: the witnesses that `f` collapses
onto `y`, kept rather than forgotten. Everything in the repo is verified under
`--safe --without-K` with zero postulates in the load-bearing tracks.
