== What is an echo type?

When a function `f : A → B` is not injective, it *loses* information: several
inputs collapse onto the same output. An *echo type* keeps a proof-relevant
record of that collapse:

[source,agda]
----
Echo f y := Σ (x : A) , (f x ≡ y)
----

`Echo f y` is the fiber of `f` over `y` — the type of inputs `x` together with
a witness that `f x ≡ y`. It is *proof-relevant*: for a non-injective `f` with
two distinct preimages of `y`, `Echo f y` is genuinely not a mere proposition.
That non-truncatability is the load-bearing distinction from frameworks that
forget the witness.

The slogan: *irreversible computation + echo = reversible representation.* The
totality completion `A ≃ Σ B (Echo f)` (the `EchoTotalCompletion` keystone)
makes that precise.

== The identity claim

echo-types advances a falsifiable claim that "echo types" name a phenomenon of
their own. The claim rests on three *decision gates* (see <<Roadmap.adoc#,Roadmap>>
and `roadmap-gates.adoc`):

. *Distinct phenomenon* — structured loss under non-injective computation is
  distinct from refinement types, lens/optic theory, HoTT fibers, setoid
  quotients, and provenance semirings.
. *Characteristic theorems* — at least two theorems are naturally echo-shaped
  and not reducible to generic Σ lemmas.
. *Canonical examples* — at least one worked example where echo types are the
  right explanatory unit.

Each gate has an explicit failure action; failures are recorded in
`docs/retractions.adoc`, not silently revised. Retractions are the *mechanism*
that makes the claim falsifiable — not a failure of the repository.

== What echo types add

* *Non-truncatable witnesses.* For non-injective `f`, the fiber retains the
  multiplicity neighbour theories propositionally truncate away (the
  `echo-not-prop` family).
* *Structured residue.* `EchoResidue` / `EchoLossTaxonomy` /
  `EchoResidueTaxonomy` classify *what* is lost and *what shape* the residue
  takes (the "kinds-of-loss × shapes-of-residue" grid).
* *A reindexing modality.* The establishment track characterises Echo as a
  *reindexing modality of structured loss* (coeffect / quantitative lineage),
  with a genuine graded-comonad side-construction (Pillar F).
* *Honest matched-negatives.* Every identity claim ships with the things it
  does *not* prove (e.g. `EchoEntropy` pairs "Shannon entropy can't
  distinguish" with "the witness can").

== What echo types do NOT add

The project's standing posture is *narrow-true over broad-easy*:

* Echo adds *no new judgment* — it is definitionally `fib`. The
  linear/dependent judgmental ladder is explicitly the *wrong* target.
* Echo is *not a resource / semiring instance*. It is an orthogonal
  indexed/residual modality: a resource algebra may *measure* Echo residues
  (the `Echo.Measure` seam), but it does not *define* Echo. Equal residue
  measure does not imply equal Echo — mechanised in
  `Echo.Separation.NotResourceInstance`, stated in `FOUNDATION_CONTRACT.md`.
  Downstream languages must not model Echo as a `Soundness(S)` resource
  instance.
* The integration recipe across the five decoration axes is *organising
  vocabulary*, not a locus of distinctness (the EI-2 finding: recipe
  commutations carry one-axis-at-a-time content, not substantive simultaneous
  interaction — terminated negatively, do not reopen).
* The full (epi, mono) image factorisation, contractible/propositional fibre
  characterisations, and the strict OFS clauses need truncation / UIP / funext;
  these are honestly *deferred earn-back gates*, not silent assumptions.
* Five retracted claims (R-2026-05-18): four were earned back at honest
  strength via Pillar F gates; the conservativity metatheorem stays retracted.
