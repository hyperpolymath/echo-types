== Structural argument summary (one paragraph for citation)

Distinctness against neighbour frameworks rests on the *truncation
argument* (`echo-not-prop` family in `proofs/agda/examples/`,
formalising that for non-injective `f` with multiple preimages of
`y`, `Echo f y` is constructively not a mere proposition) and the
*2-cell argument* (Sophisticated submodules of `EchoVsQuotient.agda`
and `EchoVsGalois.agda`, formalising that natural 2-cells in
neighbour frameworks are Σ-over-preimages-shaped). The integration
recipe is *organising vocabulary*, not the locus of distinctness.
