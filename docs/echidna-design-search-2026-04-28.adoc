== Background

Two open milestones in `docs/buchholz-plan.adoc`:

* **Phase 1.3** вҖ” monotonicity lemmas for `Ordinal.Brouwer.Arithmetic`
  (`osuc-mono-вүӨ`, `вҠ•-mono-вүӨ-right`). Blocked because the current
  `data _вүӨ_` axiomatisation makes `osuc-mono-вүӨ` non-trivial in the
  `вүӨ-lim` case.
* **Unbudgeted `wf-<бөҮКіб¶ _`** вҖ” `Ordinal.Buchholz.RecursiveSurfaceBudget`
  carries a `BudgetedBT = в„• Г— BT` budget alongside each derivation;
  the goal is `WellFounded _<бөҮКіб¶ _` directly without the budget.

These were attacked using a new Echidna capability stack (built in
this session):

. `echidna corpus ingest` вҖ” structural index of every named
  declaration in `proofs/agda/Ordinal/**.agda`, plus dependency DAG
  and axiom-hazard flags.
. `echidna design search <problem>` вҖ” simulated annealing over
  *design space* (axiomatisation shapes / rank shapes), not tactic
  space. Energy is symbolic, computed from the corpus + heuristic
  predicates.
. `echidna design swarm <problem>` вҖ” N parallel SA agents with
  cross-pollination through a coordinator. Used to validate that the
  single-chain SA result was robust.

== Phase 1.3 вҖ” recommended `_вүӨ_` redesign

=== What Echidna found

`echidna design search brouwer-leq --top 12 --iterations 2000`
agrees across all top candidates: switch `_вүӨ_` to a *recursive*
definition.

Energy `[0, 0, 1, 0]` вҖ” zero monotonicity blockers, zero K-elim
hazards, single seed constructor, recursive style.

Data-style alternatives (with `LimBelow` added) score `[2, 0, 4, 1]`
or worse; my hand-trace of `osuc-mono-вүӨ` under `data + LimBelow`
confirms the SA's verdict вҖ” in the `вүӨ-lim n q` case, neither
`вүӨ-suc вҶ’ вүӨ-lim k` nor `LimBelow` recovers a path from
`q : x вүӨ f n` to `osuc x вүӨ osuc (olim f)`. The recursive form makes
`osuc-mono-вүӨ p = p` because the equation
`osuc Оұ вүӨ osuc ОІ = Оұ вүӨ ОІ` reduces the goal definitionally.

=== Recommended axiomatisation

Sketched (proof bodies omitted вҖ” see "Proof obligations" below):

[source,agda]
----
_вүӨ_ : Ord вҶ’ Ord вҶ’ Set
oz      вүӨ _        = вҠӨ
osuc Оұ  вүӨ oz       = вҠҘ
osuc Оұ  вүӨ osuc ОІ   = Оұ вүӨ ОІ
osuc Оұ  вүӨ olim f   = ОЈ в„• О» n вҶ’ osuc Оұ вүӨ f n
olim f  вүӨ ОІ        = вҲҖ n вҶ’ f n вүӨ ОІ
----

=== Proof obligations under this axiomatisation

. `вүӨ-refl : вҲҖ {Оұ} вҶ’ Оұ вүӨ Оұ` вҖ” structural induction on `Оұ`.
. `вүӨ-step : вҲҖ {Оұ ОІ} вҶ’ Оұ вүӨ ОІ вҶ’ Оұ вүӨ osuc ОІ` вҖ” non-trivial; case-splits on
  both arguments.
. `вүӨ-trans : вҲҖ {Оұ ОІ Оі} вҶ’ Оұ вүӨ ОІ вҶ’ ОІ вүӨ Оі вҶ’ Оұ вүӨ Оі` вҖ” induct on `Оі` first
  (most reductions branch on the right argument).
. `f-in-lim : вҲҖ f n вҶ’ f n вүӨ olim f` вҖ” pattern on `f n`; for the
  successor case, supply `ОЈ` witness `(n , вүӨ-refl)`.
. `osuc-mono-вүӨ p = p` вҖ” *identity* by definitional reduction. **The
  whole point of the redesign.**
. `вҠ•-mono-вүӨ-right : вҲҖ {Оұ} {x y} вҶ’ x вүӨ y вҶ’ Оұ вҠ• x вүӨ Оұ вҠ• y` вҖ” structural
  on `Оұ` (since `_вҠ•_` is left-recursive).
. `wf-< : WellFounded _<_` вҖ” needs re-proof under the recursive
  definition. The structural-induction proof in the current
  `Brouwer.agda` uses `Acc _<_` constructed from
  `pred-of-osuc`/`pred-of-olim`; adapt those to the recursive `_вүӨ_`.

=== Risks I noticed during hand-tracing

* The `вүӨ-step` lemma вҖ” currently a single line `вүӨ-step = вүӨ-suc` вҖ” is
  non-trivial under recursive `_вүӨ_`. Specifically the case
  `Оұ = osuc Оұ', ОІ = olim f` requires `Оұ' вүӨ osuc (olim f)`, which
  case-splits to `ОЈ n. osuc Оұ' вүӨ f n` вҖ” needs careful witness picking.
* `pred-of-olim` вҖ” currently uses pattern match on `вүӨ-lim n q`; under
  recursive `_вүӨ_`, the relation isn't pattern-matchable, so this
  becomes a structural fact about `ОЈ`-elimination.

These don't block the redesign вҖ” they're well-trodden constructive
ordinal proofs in literature вҖ” but they represent real proof work,
not a one-line refactor.

== Unbudgeted `wf-<бөҮКіб¶ _` вҖ” recommended rank shape

=== What Echidna found

`echidna design search buchholz-rank --top 5` and
`echidna design swarm buchholz-rank --agents 4` both converge on the
same minimal-blocker shape with energy `[3, 0, 1]`:

[source,agda]
----
rank : BT вҶ’ Ord
rank bzero        = oz
rank (bOmega ОҪ)   = Пү-rank ОҪ
rank (bplus x y)  = rank x вҠ• rank y
rank (bpsi ОҪ Оұ)   = psi-rank ОҪ (rank Оұ)
----

The three blockers are the three `_<бөҮКіб¶ _` constructor cases of
`rank-mono`. Two of them (`<бөҮКіб¶ -ПҲОұ`, `<бөҮКіб¶ -+2`) reduce to the same
new lemma `вҠ•-mono-<-right` (a Phase-1.3 deliverable). The third
(`<бөҮКіб¶ -core`) reduces to Phase-2.2's `rank-mono-<бөҮ` for the base
Buchholz order.

=== Closing the goal

Once Phase-1.3 monotonicity and Phase-2.2 rank-mono land:

[source,agda]
----
rank-mono : вҲҖ {x y} вҶ’ x <бөҮКіб¶  y вҶ’ rank x < rank y
rank-mono (<бөҮКіб¶ -core p) = rank-mono-<бөҮ p
rank-mono (<бөҮКіб¶ -ПҲОұ   p) = -- ПҲ-strict-mono via osuc-strict-mono
                          --   on (Пү-rank ОҪ вҠ• rank Оұ)
                          --   вҹ№ вҠ•-mono-<-right
rank-mono (<бөҮКіб¶ -+2   p) = вҠ•-mono-<-right p

wf-<бөҮКіб¶  : WellFounded _<бөҮКіб¶ _
wf-<бөҮКіб¶  = Subrelation.wellFounded rank-mono
            (InverseImage.wellFounded rank wf-<)
----

(`Subrelation`, `InverseImage` from `Induction.WellFounded`. `wf-<`
is the existing Brouwer well-foundedness вҖ” itself unchanged by the
Phase-1.3 redesign so long as the redesign keeps it well-founded.)

== Proof-obligation graph

The two milestones share dependencies. In the order I'd attack them:

. **`osuc-mono-<` (strict version) and `вҠ•-mono-<-right`** in
  `Ordinal.Brouwer` + `Ordinal.Brouwer.Arithmetic`. Phase-1.3
  deliverables. Recursive `_вүӨ_` makes `osuc-mono-<` trivial; `вҠ•-mono-<-
  right` is structural on `Оұ`.
. **Re-prove `wf-<` under the recursive `_вүӨ_`.** Structural on `Ord`
  with adapted `pred-of-osuc` / `pred-of-olim`.
. **`rank-mono-<бөҮ : x <бөҮ y вҶ’ rank x < rank y`** in a new
  `Ordinal.Buchholz.Rank` module. Phase-2.2 deliverable. Constructor-
  by-constructor induction over the 13-shape admitted core of `_<бөҮ_`.
. **Drop `RecursiveSurfaceBudget`** in favour of direct
  `wf-<бөҮКіб¶  : WellFounded _<бөҮКіб¶ _` via the `Subrelation` chain. The
  budget infrastructure becomes vestigial.

== SA energy assumptions (for sanity-checking)

The SA's energy is symbolic, not from running Agda. Two predicates
might be wrong; spot-check before fully trusting the recommendation:

* **`mono_osuc_ok` (in `learning/design_search.rs`)**: claims that
  `_вүӨ_` is `Recursive` *or* contains `вүӨ-cong-suc` вҹ№ `osuc-mono-вүӨ`
  is trivial. I verified by hand for the `Recursive` case. The
  `вүӨ-cong-suc` claim is also straightforward (apply the constructor).
  No data-style + `LimBelow` path is recognised вҖ” confirmed by hand
  trace.
* **`blockers` (in `learning/buchholz_rank.rs`)**: claims that
  `<бөҮКіб¶ -+2` always needs `вҠ•-mono-<-right`, regardless of whether
  `bplus` rank is `LeftSum` or `OsucSum`. The `OsucSum` case
  technically needs `osuc-strict-mono вҲҳ вҠ•-mono-<-right` вҖ” the outer
  `osuc` is still strict-monotonic, so the dependency reduces to
  `вҠ•-mono-<-right` either way. Confirmed.
