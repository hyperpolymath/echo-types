= Echo types — what it is, and what we did not invent
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 2

_An upfront, scholarly statement of the object: the established mathematics it
instantiates, the boundary of our own contribution, and the relations it is
NOT. Written to be read alongside (and never to contradict) this repo's
`AFFIRMATION.adoc` ground truth._

toc::[]

== What it is (as we use it)

In this repository, an *echo type* is a *min-plus-graded modality of structured
information loss*: a way to talk about a value that has crossed a non-injective
map — a degradation, projection, observation, compression, or translation — and
survived not as the original guarantee, and not as nothing, but as a *retained,
proof-relevant constraint on what its origin could have been*.

Operationally, in the actual Agda kernel, the object is exact and small. Given
`f : A → B`, the echo at `y : B` is the *preimage / homotopy fibre*

----
Echo f y := Σ (x : A) , (f x ≡ y)
----

defined in `proofs/agda/Echo.agda` and used throughout the verified closure
(`proofs/agda/All.agda`). It adds *no new judgment*: `Echo f y` is definitionally
the fibre, pinned by the bridge `echo↔fib : Echo f y ↔ fiber f y`
(`EchoFiberBridge.agda`). On top of this single definition the repo builds the
modal reading used in practice:

* a *graded modality* `D_r` indexed by a *loss grade* `r`, where the grade lives
  in the *tropical (min-plus) semiring* `(ℕ ∪ {∞}, min, +)` read as *structural
  irrecoverability* — `0` = nothing lost, `∞` = total irrecoverable loss. The
  grade carrier and its operations (`_+g_` = additive accumulation, `_⊓g_` =
  min/join) are mechanised in `proofs/agda/experimental/echo-additive/Grade.agda`;
* a *section/retraction* discipline whose round-trip is *exact on a homotopy
  fibre* (the recoverable subobject) rather than over-approximating or
  storing a complement — this is the canonical *total-completion equivalence*
  `A ≃ Σ B (Echo f)` (`EchoTotalCompletion.agda`), one leg of an
  (equivalence, projection) orthogonal factorisation system
  (`EchoOrthogonalFactorizationSystem.agda`);
* a *residue-measure observation seam* (`Echo.Measure.Interface`): a resource
  algebra (cost / tropical / confidence) may *measure* an echo residue, but the
  measure is a lossy observation of the modality, not the modality itself.

So "echo type" as we use it = *the fibre, read as a tropically-graded loss
modality with an exact-on-a-fibre recoverable core*. The variance of that
modality is *monadic*: loss *accumulates* (the composite of two losses is the
sum of grades), and there is no general way back from total loss.

== The established structure it instantiates (and where it is from)

Echo types are an *assembly of established structures*. None of the underlying
mathematics is ours.

*Graded (parametric) monads / graded modal types.* The "grade an effect/modality
by an element of an ordered algebraic structure, with a composition law on
grades" pattern is the theory of *graded monads* and *graded/coeffectful modal
types*. Canonical references: Katsumata, _Parametric effect monads and
semantics of effect systems_ (POPL 2014); Fujii, Katsumata and Melliès,
_Towards a formal theory of graded monads_ (FoSSaCS 2016); the coeffect /
quantitative-modality line of Petricek, Orchard and Mycroft (the coeffect
calculus papers), realised in the Granule language by Orchard, Liepelt and Eades,
_Quantitative program reasoning with graded modal types_ (ICFP 2019). The dual
comonadic reading of context-dependent computation is Uustalu and Vene,
_Comonadic notions of computation_ (ENTCS, 2008). Our grade index instantiates
this machinery at the *tropical / min-plus dioid* — itself standard idempotent /
tropical semiring theory.

*The homotopy fibre.* `Echo f y := Σ (x : A), (f x ≡ y)` is exactly the
*homotopy fibre* of `f` at `y`, and the equivalence `A ≃ Σ_{y} fib f y` with
the (equivalence, projection) factorisation are standard univalent-foundations
constructions. Canonical reference: The Univalent Foundations Program,
_Homotopy Type Theory: Univalent Foundations of Mathematics_ (2013) — the
HoTT Book — for fibres, the total-space equivalence, and orthogonal
factorisation systems.

In short: the *grade* is graded-monad / coeffect theory over a tropical dioid;
the *carrier* is the homotopy fibre. Both are decades-established; we cite them
as the load-bearing prior art, not as background colour.

== What is ours, and what is not (the contribution boundary)

What is *borrowed* is the mathematics named above: graded monads / graded modal
types, the tropical (min-plus) dioid, and the homotopy fibre with its
total-space factorisation. We claim none of it.

What is *ours* is the *modelling and assembly*: the decision to read the fibre
*as* a tropically-graded loss modality whose grade is *irrecoverability*, whose
round-trip is *exact on the fibre* (not over-approximating), and whose variance
is settled *monadically* by mechanised proof rather than asserted; and the
fitting of that model to concrete domains (database provenance, region-exit /
capability security, sampling support, sensitivity tracking, BEAM/typed-wasm
boundaries). The usefulness is reported only where it has already produced
*checkable artefacts*, grounded in this repo's `AFFIRMATION.adoc`:

* a *green `--safe --without-K` verified closure* — `agda --safe --without-K -i
  proofs/agda proofs/agda/All.agda` exits 0 over 151 forced modules, with *zero
  postulates* and a *funext-free kernel certificate* (`scripts/kernel-guard.sh`
  exits 0);
* the *deniability / no-section* proofs — `EchoDeniability.agda`,
  `EchoNoSectionGeneric.no-section-of-collapsing-map` — that make "you cannot
  reconstruct the source from the residue" a checked theorem, not a slogan;
* the *anti-collapse separation* theorems `equal-measure-does-not-imply-equal-echo`
  and `measure-not-injective` (`Echo.Separation.NotResourceInstance`, pinned in
  `Smoke.agda`), which mechanise the boundary "a residue measure does not define
  Echo";
* the executable *`EchoTypes.jl`* falsifier (finite-domain shadow,
  falsifies-by-counterexample), and the *typed-wasm L-Echo axis* as a consuming
  application.

**On contribution (the same stance in every sibling repo).** We did not invent the
mathematics this work rests on. Graded monads, tropical / idempotent dioids,
multiparty session types and choreographies, and epistemic / justification logic
are established structures authored by others, cited above. What is ours is the
*modelling*: we apply these structures as instruments fitted to specific domains —
verifiable WebAssembly, federated identity, BEAM sandboxing, structured
information loss — in places the established corpus had not been instantiated this
way, and we report only where the fit has already produced *checkable artefacts*.
The contribution is the model and its demonstrated usefulness, not the underlying
mathematics. This is deliberate, and it is a strength: a claim that concedes its own
foundations is survivable; one that claims invention dies at the first citation. (Cf. the
estate discipline — echo-types' own distinctness claim was permitted to terminate
negatively, EI-2.)

== Is-not, and what is still open

*IS-NOT relations (kept crisp so the boundaries do not erode).*

* *Echo IS-NOT a graded comonad.* The "graded comonad of structured loss"
  reading was *retracted* (R-2026-05-18) and stands retracted. The variance is
  *monadic*: the composition `μ : D_r (D_s A) → D_{r+s} A` exists (loss
  accumulates), but the would-be comonad counit `D_∞ A → A` is *mechanically
  absurd* — you cannot recover a value from total irrecoverable loss. A graded
  *comonad* structure survives *only* on restricted *content-faithful* carriers
  (Gate F1, the iterated-residue line); on the inert ⊤-padded carrier its laws
  pass only *vacuously* (the discharge is by contractibility, not by content),
  and that vacuity is itself recorded as the variance finding. This whole
  variance settlement lives in the *gated experimental tree*
  (`proofs/agda/experimental/echo-additive/`), *not* in the main verified
  closure.
* *Echo IS-NOT a tropical / resource instance.* A resource algebra may *measure*
  an echo residue (cost, tropical, confidence), but it does not *define* Echo:
  *equal residue measure does not imply equal Echo* (mechanised, above). Echo is
  an orthogonal indexed/residual modality; do not model it as a resource-algebra
  `Soundness(S)` instance. Vocabulary discipline: *resource grade* ≠ *echo index*
  ≠ *residue measure*; avoid "echo-grade".
* *Echo IS-NOT a new judgment or a replacement for exact-preservation systems.*
  It adds no judgment to the type theory (it is definitionally the fibre), and it
  is not a substitute for ordinary type checking, ABI/FFI proofs, typed-wasm, or
  any boundary system that *can* preserve exact guarantees. When exact
  preservation is available, use it; echo types are for the case where the value
  that crossed is no longer the guarantee but is also not meaningless.
* *Echo's distinctness claim was NOT assumed.* It was put to a falsifier and
  *terminated negatively* (EI-2, via PATH B): the cross-axis simultaneous-
  integration distinctness was not established, and the Gate-1 claim was narrowed
  accordingly. We keep that negative on the record rather than spin it.

*Honestly open.*

* The *variance settlement* (graded monad in general; graded comonad only on
  content-faithful carriers) is proved *only in the gated experimental tree* and
  is *not wired into* the shipped `--safe` closure (`Grade.agda` and the
  `echo-additive/` modules are absent from `All.agda`). Treat the variance result
  as a real-but-unwired artefact, exactly as `AFFIRMATION.adoc` records it.
* *Bachmann–Howard order-type fidelity is POSTULATED*, in
  `Ordinal/Buchholz/Fidelity.agda`, *outside* the `--safe` kernel cone. The
  ordinal-notation tower's sound-carrier well-foundedness is proved and in the
  closure; the bridge from that notation to the Bachmann–Howard ordinal is an
  explicit, auditable trust boundary, not a closed theorem. "Mechanized
  Bachmann–Howard" is a milestone-in-progress, read as "a notation system aimed
  at BH", not "BH proved".
* The *truncated (epi, mono) image factorisation* needs propositional truncation
  and lives in a separate, unwired postulated module; the safe core proves only
  the K-free `injective-fibres-proj-unique` fragment. Several "full" universal-
  property clauses are honestly *funext-/UIP-qualified* (Gates F4/F5), stated as
  conditional, not unconditional.
