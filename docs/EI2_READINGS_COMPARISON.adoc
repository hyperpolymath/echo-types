== What happened

This document was originally drafted to compare two candidate
gate-1 amendments:

* READING 1 (strict): accept ModeGrade as falsifier, weaken gate-1.
* READING 2 (refined): make non-loss-only an explicit recipe
  precondition, gate-1 narrows but holds.

Both readings assumed the non-loss-only criterion was *sufficient*
for substantive simultaneous cross-axis interaction. The prior
session's `RoleRole.agda` work refuted this: even two non-loss-only
axes paired together (Choreo × Choreo) produce trivial coordinate-
product commutation in the independent-product design, and break
per-axis transport in the shared-state design. NLO is *necessary
but not sufficient* for substantive simultaneous interaction; in
fact, no axis pairing within the existing recipe produces
substantive simultaneous content.
